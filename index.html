lorenzo's Poetry

More than a friend you are
you are my diary of sort
a place where i store my life's events
the first person i wish to tell anything worth telling

SEUN

weird, one word one Syllable
i see her coming and i get confused like i'm a weirdo
she smart, she beautiful, she like the devil
no evil just that strong dark complexion

i can't sleep
i wake up at night and can't return to sleep
i close my eyes but still no sleep
she comes to me at night,
like a shadow she creaps into my mind
i try to fight to go to sleep
i tried and tried but still no sleep
but then i told her of all i'll do
of tales of sun and tales of moon
and after words to sleep return

how fairs the queen and the young princes and princessa
for i miss that chunk of chocolate sweetness,the Lady Bell
and the cute prince of steez,Lord Marvel
oh what joy would such pumpkins rain on the lord and Lady for birthing such treasures
for it is only fitting that treasures be formed from great marbles
Bless your union and the lord for anointing it
and bless us all for being an extention of your happiness
